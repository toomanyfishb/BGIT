Classic Chocolate Cake

The recipe ingredients:

  • 1¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 1½ tsp baking powder
  • 1½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  4. Pour into prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  5. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove to wire racks to cool completely.
Zesty Lemon Tart

Ingredients:

  • 1 pastry crust (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • ⅔ cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp lemon zest
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Place the pastry crust in a 9-inch tart pan and press down the edges.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together sugar, eggs, and egg yolks until smooth.
  3. Stir in lemon juice, lemon zest, and melted butter.
  4. Pour the mixture into the crust and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until filling is set.
  5. Allow to cool completely before serving. Garnish with whipped cream or fresh berries if desired.

Ingredients:

  • ¾ cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ¾ tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2¾ cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup raisins

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). In a large bowl, cream together butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until smooth.
  2. Beat in eggs and vanilla until fluffy.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt. Gradually beat into the butter mixture.
  4. Stir in oats and raisins.
  5. Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  6. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until golden brown. Cool slightly on cookie sheets, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.
